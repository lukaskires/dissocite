An Introduction

December 16 · 2025

Hi,

Welcome to my website. I'm a neuroscience graduate student, and I've created this space to write essays, share information, and review books and films. I post short-form content on X and publish longer-form writing here.

Essays

Here, I write essays that explore topics such as science, politics, philosophy, music, health, and nutrition.

Information

Each month, I publish an article titled Words, Things, and Ideas, where I share and explain words, things, and ideas I've recently encountered and found interesting or unfamiliar. Each entry typically consists of a list of ten items, followed by a short piece of writing that weaves them together.

Reviews

I'm building a growing collection of book and movie reviews, updated monthly with the titles I've read or watched. At the moment, only some entries include reviews, but I'm in the process of adding them to all items. When a book or film resonates particularly strongly or sparks more extensive reflection, I may write a longer-form essay review.
